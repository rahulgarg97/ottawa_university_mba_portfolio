Portfolio Overview

This website includes one major artifact from each core MBA course. For each course, I included a short reflection on the assignment, the learning outcomes it connects to, an embedded document viewer, and a readable text version so the work can be reviewed directly on the page.

Together, these artifacts show how my learning developed across organizational behavior, ethics, economics, finance, accounting, and strategy.

Core Course Artifacts

BUS 7000

Organizational Behavior and Theory

Artifact: Leveraging Social Capital to Obtain Top Management Support in Complex, Cross-Functional IT Projects

In this assignment, I practiced applying organizational behavior concepts to a realistic IT project challenge. The project helped me see that cross-functional success depends not only on the technical plan, but also on trust, communication, leadership support, and relationships across departments.

BUS 7200

Value Systems and Professional Ethics

Artifact: Erosion of Safety Culture at Boeing Case Study Analysis

In this case study, I analyzed the Boeing 737 MAX crisis through ethics, governance, stakeholder responsibility, and safety culture. The assignment helped me understand how business pressure and ethical responsibility can conflict, especially in industries where human safety is involved.

View artifact page
BUS 7500

Managerial Economics

Artifact: Evaluating the Profitability of Implementing Remote Work Policies Post-Pandemic

In this project, I used managerial economics to evaluate whether a structured hybrid work policy could create long-term value. I looked at costs, productivity, retention, implementation expenses, and risks before recommending a practical workplace model.

View artifact page
BUS 7600

Managerial Finance

Artifact: ExxonMobil vs. Chevron Final Written Project

In this finance project, I compared ExxonMobil and Chevron using financial ratios, DuPont analysis, discounted cash flow, and dividend valuation. The assignment helped me move beyond reporting numbers and practice using financial evidence to support an investment recommendation.

In this accounting project, I worked with a Balanced Scorecard proposal for Ragland Department Store.
